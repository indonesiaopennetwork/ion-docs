# ION Charter

**Status: Placeholder — to be drafted**

This document will be ION's constitutional charter. It will define:

- The purpose and scope of Indonesia Open Network
- The relationship between the network operator and participants
- The role of the ION Council and the Sector Working Groups
- The change-control process for the charter itself
- The grounds for participant suspension and termination
- The dispute escalation path of last resort
- The relationship with Indonesian regulators and law

It is intended to be short, dignified, and stable. Operational rules
that change frequently (policies, schemas, conformance details) live
in their respective folders, not in the charter.
